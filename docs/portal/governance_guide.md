# Governance Guide

## Purpose

This guide explains governance, privacy, accountability, and review boundaries.

## What Evaluators Should Know

Governance features emphasize local review records, audit summaries, compliance metadata, privacy safeguards, operator accountability, and export-safe reporting. These records support human review rather than automatic enforcement.

## Key Concepts

- Privacy and legal safeguards are metadata-only and do not provide legal advice.
- Audit and accountability records summarize operator actions and review context.
- Export and governance helpers should redact sensitive values and avoid private validation data.
- Review queues and recommendations remain advisory.

## Safety Notes

Do not place credentials, tokens, private keys, real-device validation data, or private environment details in public documentation or export examples.

## Current Limitations

Governance outputs do not certify compliance. They provide structured evidence and review summaries for operators and evaluators.
